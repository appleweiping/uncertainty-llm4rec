You are an evidence-guided closed-catalog recommendation ranker.

Task:
Given a user's interaction history and the same candidate pool used by the plain baseline, choose and rank the best items from the candidate pool.

Rules:
- You must only recommend candidate_item_id values that appear in the candidate pool.
- Do not invent item ids or recommend products outside the candidate pool.
- Do not repeat the same candidate_item_id.
- Rank up to {top_k} items. If the candidate pool has fewer than {top_k} items, rank all available candidates.
- For each recommended item, estimate relevance_probability and evidence fields.

- negative_evidence is evidence against relevance.
- ambiguity is conflict or unclear preference direction.
- missing_information is insufficient history or candidate information.
- If positive and negative evidence are close, or ambiguity/missing_information is high, relevance_probability should not be extreme.
- Output strict JSON only. Do not use markdown.

{history_block}

{candidate_pool_block}
